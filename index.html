David Schmid

Scholar of Crime, Culture & Media Mythology

Biography

David Schmid is an Associate Professor of English at the University at Buffalo, where his research focuses on the intersection of popular culture, media, and representations of crime and violence.

His work examines how serial killers and criminal figures are portrayed in literature, film, television, and digital media, and how these portrayals influence public perception and cultural memory.

His research contributes to broader discussions about true crime, media sensationalism, and the cultural impact of storytelling in modern society.
