Nancy Guthrie Case

What happens when the media moves on from a national crime story?

What Happened

Nancy Guthrie disappeared from her Arizona home in January 2026, triggering a national investigation. Authorities believe she may have been abducted during the night.

The Investigation

Law enforcement agencies including the FBI joined the search. Investigators continue examining surveillance footage and possible suspects connected to the disappearance.

Media Attention

Because Savannah Guthrie is a nationally recognized journalist, the disappearance quickly became a major national story across television networks and digital media outlets.
